## 4. Using the Provider App — Driver Operations

### 4.1 Going Online and Receiving Requests

You go online by toggling the online/offline switch in the Provider App. While online, your GPS location is broadcast to the matching system to receive Ride requests in your area.

### 4.2 Accepting and Declining Requests

You may accept or decline incoming Ride requests within the displayed acceptance window. We do not require you to accept any specific request, but consistently low acceptance rates may affect your matching priority and may be grounds for review.

### 4.3 Toggle Lock During Active Rides

Once a Ride is in **accepted, en route, or in progress** status, the online/offline toggle is locked to "online" in the App. This prevents premature deactivation and protects the Client. Force-closing the App during an active Ride is treated as a connectivity drop (see Section 4.7).

### 4.4 Multi-Stop Rides

Clients may add stops at booking or during the Ride. Fares recalculate accordingly. **You may decline a mid-ride stop** that is outside your service area or outside the Ashanti pilot region; in that case, the Ride continues to the original destination.

### 4.5 Cancellation Policy

You may cancel an accepted Ride before pickup. **Three (3) cancellations within a rolling 30-day period** triggers a temporary suspension pending admin review. A cancellation reason is required and recorded.

### 4.6 Surge Pricing

We may apply surge multipliers in periods of high demand. Surge applies to **new requests** only — a Ride already booked is locked at its confirmed fare, regardless of subsequent surge changes.

### 4.7 Disconnection Policy

If you lose connectivity during an active Ride, a **2-minute grace period** begins. If connectivity is restored within 2 minutes, the Ride continues. If connectivity is not restored within 2 minutes (including where you have force-closed the App), the matter is escalated to admin for manual intervention. Repeated disconnection incidents may be grounds for review.

### 4.8 Code of Conduct

You agree to:

- Be courteous and respectful at all times;
- Drive safely and lawfully, complying with all Ghanaian traffic laws;
- Not be under the influence of alcohol or any impairing substance during a Ride;
- Not smoke in the vehicle while a passenger is present;
- Take the most direct safe route to the destination, save where the Client requests otherwise;
- Not solicit Clients for off-platform rides for journeys originated on MyShop;
- Not discriminate against any Client on grounds of ethnicity, religion, gender, disability, or any other protected characteristic;
- Not refuse a Ride to a Client with a service animal or a person with disabilities, except where the law expressly permits refusal;
- Comply with the masked-call/masked-chat communication rules — never share your real phone number with Clients on the Platform;
- Activate the emergency button only in genuine emergencies.

### 4.9 Prohibited Activities

You agree NOT to:

- Use any vehicle other than the registered, approved vehicle for the Ride;
- Permit any unauthorised person to use your Driver Account;
- Carry passengers in excess of the legal seating capacity of the vehicle;
- Carry unlawful items;
- Manipulate the GPS, fare meter, or any Platform feature;
- Solicit cash payments outside the agreed fare or take any tip not given voluntarily;
- Engage in any activity that brings the Platform into disrepute.

## 5. Payment, Commission, and Payouts

### 5.1 Fares

Ride fares are calculated by the Platform on the basis of time and distance, plus any applicable surcharges (surge multiplier, additional stops). Fractional fares are rounded up to the nearest whole Ghana Cedi.

### 5.2 Commission

Gilmore Technologies deducts a **platform commission of 20%** from the gross fare of each completed Ride. Commission is calculated on the **pre-promo fare** — that is, the fare before any client-side promo code or discount is applied. The remaining 80% is paid out to you as your earnings.

### 5.3 Gateway Fees

Flutterwave gateway fees are absorbed by the Platform within the 20% commission — you are not charged separately for gateway fees.

### 5.4 Payout Options

You may choose between:

- **Instant payout:** earnings transferred to your registered MoMo wallet within approximately 30–60 seconds of dual-confirmed Ride completion; or
- **End-of-day batch payout:** all earnings for the day paid in a single transfer at 18:00 GMT.

Both options are **free of charge** to you. You may change your payout preference in the Provider App at any time.

### 5.5 Tips

Tips are processed separately. **No commission is deducted from tips.** Tips are paid to your wallet via the same payout method you have selected.

### 5.6 Wallet and Disputes

- Where a Client raises a dispute, only the disputed Ride's amount is frozen pending resolution; your other earnings remain accessible.
- If a refund is approved after you have already been paid, the refunded amount may be **clawed back from your next payout**, with notice to you and a clear explanation.

### 5.7 Clawback and Outstanding Balances

- Your account cannot be deactivated or soft-deleted while an outstanding clawback balance exists.
- Clawback balances under **GHS 100** on accounts inactive for more than 90 days may be written off by the Platform.
- Clawback balances **above GHS 100** on inactive accounts will be escalated for manual resolution and may include direct contact and/or referral to a collections process.

### 5.8 Tax

You are solely responsible for declaring and paying all taxes (income tax, VAT where applicable, levies) and statutory contributions on your earnings. We may issue earnings statements to assist your reporting and may share data with the Ghana Revenue Authority where required by law.

### 5.9 Foreign Currency Card Settlement

Where a Client pays by a non-GHS card, foreign exchange charges may apply at the gateway level. During the pilot, the Platform absorbs these charges within commission. Post-pilot policy may change with notice.

### 5.10 Batch Payout Failures

If the daily batch payout fails at 18:00 GMT, automatic retries occur at 19:30, 20:00, and 06:00 the following morning. If all retries fail, admin will be alerted and you will be notified — funds will not silently roll into the next day's batch.

## 6. Safety and Emergency Features

### 6.1 Emergency Button

The Provider App includes an emergency button for use during any active Ride. Activation requires two-step confirmation. On confirmation, the Platform may simultaneously share your live GPS location with admin and registered emergency contact(s), place an automated call to Ghana Police Service (191), and trigger live audio/video recording uploaded to secure cloud storage.

### 6.2 False Activations

False or non-emergency activations of the emergency button are a serious breach. We may suspend or terminate your account and seek to recover associated costs, including police call-out costs.

### 6.3 Phone Number Masking

All communication with Clients on the Platform is routed through masked numbers. **You must never share your real phone number with a Client through the Platform**, and you must not solicit Clients to communicate or transact off-platform.

## 7. Performance, Ratings, and Standards

### 7.1 Ratings

Clients rate you after each Ride. Ratings are revealed after both parties submit, or after 24 hours has elapsed (the "blind rating window"), whichever is sooner.

### 7.2 Acceptance and Cancellation Rates

We monitor your acceptance rate, cancellation rate, and completion rate. Sustained poor performance may result in reduced matching priority, additional training requirements, or account suspension.

### 7.3 Cancellation Threshold

**Three (3) cancellations within a rolling 30-day period** triggers temporary suspension pending admin review.

### 7.4 Rating Threshold

- A rating below **3.5 stars** (calculated over a minimum of 15 completed Rides) triggers an in-app warning.
- A rating below **3.0 stars** (over a minimum of 15 completed Rides) triggers automatic suspension pending admin review.

### 7.5 Rating Integrity

Ratings procured through inducement, intimidation, or off-platform pressure are a breach of these Terms and may be invalidated.
