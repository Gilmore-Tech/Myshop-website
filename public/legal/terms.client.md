## 3. Using the Platform

### 3.1 Ride-Hailing Services

**3.1.1 Booking a Ride.** You may request a Ride by entering your destination, reviewing the estimated fare and pickup time, and confirming your booking. The fare you see at booking is an **estimate**, calculated from time and distance; the **final fare** is determined at the end of the trip. Fractional fares are rounded up to the nearest whole Ghana Cedi.

**3.1.2 Multi-Stop Rides.** You may add additional stops at booking or during the Ride. Adding stops will recalculate the fare. Drivers may decline mid-ride stops outside their service area or outside the Ashanti pilot region; in such cases, the Ride continues to your original destination.

**3.1.3 Cancellation of Rides.** You may cancel a Ride free of charge **within 3 minutes** of the Driver accepting your request. After 3 minutes, a cancellation fee applies and will be charged to your selected payment method.

**3.1.4 Surge Pricing.** At times of high demand, surge pricing may apply. Surge multipliers are clearly displayed before you confirm. Surge changes that occur **after** you confirm a booking do not affect your existing booking — the fare you confirmed is the fare that applies.

### 3.2 Artisan Services

**3.2.1 Requesting a Service.** You may request an artisan service by selecting a category, describing the job, optionally uploading photos, choosing your location, and setting a preferred time (immediate or scheduled). If no Artisans are available in the requested category in your area, we will inform you immediately and notify you when one becomes available.

**3.2.2 Bidding Window.** Up to three (3) Artisans may submit Bids for your job within a five (5) minute window. You may select your preferred Artisan from the bids received. If no Bids are received, we may manually assign an available Artisan.

**3.2.3 Understanding Bids.** Each Bid is a **single total amount** intended to cover both labour and the materials the Artisan estimates the job will require. Artisans use professional experience to estimate this total. Bids below a category-specific minimum or unusually high (above GHS 5,000) may be moderated by us before being shown to you.

**3.2.4 Material Cost Supplement Requests.** Where an Artisan discovers on arrival that the job requires materially more materials than originally estimated, the Artisan may submit **one (1) supplement request** before commencing work, which you must approve or reject in-app:

- If you **approve**, the agreed price is increased by the supplement amount.
- If you **reject**, the Artisan may proceed at the original price (doing what is reasonably possible within scope) or you may both agree to cancel. Disputes will be mediated by our admin team.
- **Only one supplement request is permitted per job.** No further supplements can be raised after one has been submitted.

**3.2.5 Cancellation of Artisan Jobs.** You may cancel a confirmed job free of charge **within 30 minutes** of selecting an Artisan, provided the Artisan has not yet marked themselves "en route". After 30 minutes, or after the Artisan has marked en route (whichever is sooner), a cancellation fee equal to **20% of the agreed price** is payable to the Artisan.

**3.2.6 Confirming Job Completion.** After the Artisan marks the job complete, you have a window to confirm completion in-app. **If you do not confirm within four (4) hours**, the Artisan may escalate to our admin team. We may, after reviewing evidence (photos, chat history, timestamps), force-release payment if we determine the job was completed as agreed.

**3.2.7 Scheduled Job Reminders.** For jobs scheduled in advance, you will receive reminder notifications 24 hours and 2 hours before the scheduled time. If the Artisan does not confirm or arrive on time, the system may escalate the job and we will work to find a replacement.

### 3.3 USSD Channel

The USSD Channel allows you to request artisan services on any mobile phone, without an internet connection. USSD users acknowledge: sessions are limited to 180 seconds (telco standard); location is set by zone selection; USSD payments are MoMo only (MTN, Telecel, AirtelTigo); notifications are delivered by SMS; USSD bookings are subject to the same Terms as App bookings, except where the channel imposes practical limitations.

### 3.4 Communication

During an active Ride or Job, you may communicate with the Provider via in-app chat or masked phone call. **Both parties' real phone numbers remain hidden** from each other. Communication channels close automatically when the Booking is marked complete.

## 5. Disputes and Refunds

### 5.1 Raising a Dispute

You may raise a dispute about a Ride or Artisan Job within **two (2) hours** of completion. Disputes raised after this window will not normally be reviewed except in exceptional circumstances.

### 5.2 How Disputes Are Handled

Our admin team reviews evidence including GPS trail data, photos, chat logs, and timestamps. We may refund all or part of the amount paid, adjust the agreed price, take action against a Provider, or decline the dispute where the Provider performed as agreed.

### 5.3 Ride Fare Disputes

Where you dispute a Ride fare on the basis that the route was inefficient, we will compare the actual GPS trail against the optimal route. If the actual route exceeds the optimal route by **more than 30%** in distance (excluding documented detours), we may issue a partial refund covering the excess.

### 5.4 Refund Method

Approved refunds are credited back to the original payment method where possible, or to your in-app wallet. MoMo or card refunds may take 1–7 business days to appear.

### 5.5 Final Decisions

Our admin team's decisions on disputes are final on the Platform, without prejudice to your statutory rights under Ghanaian law.
