## 2. Eligibility and Artisan Registration

### 2.1 Eligibility

To register as an Artisan on MyShop, you must:

- Be at least **18 years of age**;
- Have legal capacity to enter binding contracts under Ghanaian law;
- Have the right to work in Ghana;
- Hold demonstrable competence in your trade;
- Pass the verification and background-check process below;
- Comply with any category-specific licensing or regulatory requirements (for example, where electrical work requires registration with the Energy Commission).

### 2.2 Required Verification Documents

You must submit, and warrant the truthfulness of, the following:

- A trade certificate or proof of qualification in your category;
- Business registration documents (where you operate as a registered business);
- A valid Ghana Card (national ID);
- A portfolio of past work — minimum **three (3) clear photos**;
- A clear profile photo of yourself;
- Written consent for a Ghana Police Criminal Records Bureau background check.

### 2.3 Verification Process

Verification proceeds in two parallel streams:

- **Digital KYC** (Smile Identity or equivalent) — typically completes within minutes;
- **Ghana Police Criminal Records Bureau check** — typically takes 2–4 weeks.

You may begin accepting Jobs once your digital KYC and document verification are approved by our admin team. **If the Ghana Police background check later returns disqualifying information, your account will be immediately suspended pending admin review and may be permanently terminated.**

### 2.4 Disqualifying Offences

Disqualifying offences include, without limitation: violent crimes; fraud; sexual offences; offences involving minors; theft; and any offence that, in our reasonable judgement, makes you unsuitable to enter clients' homes or workplaces. The full and current list is published in our Artisan Operations Handbook _[to be finalised by legal counsel]_.

### 2.5 One Artisan Account per Person

You may hold only one Artisan Account on MyShop. Attempts to create duplicate Artisan Accounts (including by using a different SIM or device) will be blocked.

### 2.6 Re-Verification

We may require re-submission of documents at any time, including where:

- A document, certificate, or licence expires;
- We receive complaints or your rating drops below the threshold in Section 8.3;
- We have reasonable grounds to suspect fraud or document forgery;
- A change in law or our policies requires updated documentation.

### 2.7 Document Fraud

Submission of forged or fraudulent documents is a material breach. We may suspend or terminate your account, claw back earnings linked to fraudulent verification, and refer the matter to law enforcement.

## 4. Bidding, Quoting, and Materials

### 4.1 The Bid Window

Where a Client requests a service in your category, the system may invite up to three (3) Artisans to bid, within a **five-minute window**. Once three Bids are received, the window closes and no further Bids are accepted.

### 4.2 What a Bid Is

Your Bid is a **single total amount** intended to cover both your labour and the materials you reasonably estimate the Job will require. You may add a short message to the Client explaining what is included.

By submitting a Bid, you make a professional commitment to perform the described Job, in good and workmanlike manner, at that price, subject only to the supplement provisions in Section 4.4 below.

### 4.3 Minimum and Maximum Bid Limits

- **Minimum bids:** each category has a platform-defined minimum bid amount. Bids below the minimum will be rejected automatically.
- **Maximum bids:** Bids above **GHS 5,000** are flagged for admin review before being shown to the Client.

### 4.4 Material Cost Supplement Requests

If, on arrival, you discover that the Job genuinely requires materially more materials than you originally estimated, you may submit a **single supplement request** before commencing work, explaining what additional materials are needed and why they were not foreseeable from the original description.

The Client must approve or reject the supplement in-app:

- If the Client **approves**, the agreed price is increased by the supplement amount and you proceed.
- If the Client **rejects**, you may either proceed at the original price (doing what is reasonably possible within scope) or both parties may agree to cancel. Disputes will be mediated by our admin team.

**Critical rules — read carefully:**

- **Only one (1) supplement request is permitted per Job.** Once a supplement is submitted (whether approved or rejected), no further supplements can be raised for the same Job.
- The supplement request must be submitted **before you begin work**. Supplements raised mid-job or after completion will be rejected.
- The supplement amount and reason must be honest and proportionate.

### 4.5 Supplement Pattern Tracking

We track supplement frequency per Artisan. **A pattern of repeated supplements relative to your completed jobs may be flagged for admin review and may result in account suspension.** Submitting low Bids with the intention of supplementing later is a serious breach.

## 5. Performing the Job

### 5.1 Communication

During an active Job you may communicate with the Client via in-app chat or masked phone call. **Real phone numbers are never disclosed** to either party. You must not solicit the Client to communicate or transact off-platform.

### 5.2 Status Updates

You are required to keep Job status accurate in the App, including:

- **En route:** when you set off for the Client's location;
- **Arrived:** when you reach the Client's location;
- **In progress:** when you commence work;
- **Complete:** when work is finished.

Status manipulation (e.g. marking arrived when you have not arrived, or marking complete when work is not complete) is a serious breach.

### 5.3 In-Home Welfare Check

Where you mark "arrived" but do not mark "complete" or send any other App activity for more than three (3) hours, the system will send an automated welfare-check push notification asking you to confirm that you are okay. **If you do not respond within fifteen (15) minutes, our admin team will be alerted with your last known GPS location and Job details.** This is intended to protect you. You consent to this welfare-check process.

### 5.4 Job Staleness

A Job that remains in "in progress" status for more than:

- **8 hours:** triggers a check-in notification to both parties;
- **24 hours:** auto-escalates to the admin queue;
- **48 hours:** results in payout being frozen and the Job flagged for manual admin review.

You agree to keep Jobs moving in good faith and not to leave them in pending states for the purpose of avoiding scrutiny or payment workflows.

### 5.5 Quality of Work

You agree to perform every Job:

- In a good and workmanlike manner;
- To the standard a reasonably skilled Artisan in your category would meet;
- Using suitable materials and tools;
- In compliance with applicable Ghanaian safety, building, electrical, plumbing, and other regulatory standards;
- Honouring any reasonable warranties implied by your trade and the agreed scope.

### 5.6 Property and Premises

You agree to respect the Client's premises:

- Take reasonable care to avoid damage;
- Clean up after work where appropriate;
- Not enter areas not necessary for the Job;
- Treat household members with respect, including children;
- Not photograph or record any part of the premises beyond what is needed for portfolio/job documentation, and only with the Client's express consent.

### 5.7 Code of Conduct

You agree to:

- Be courteous and professional at all times;
- Not be under the influence of alcohol or any impairing substance during a Job;
- Not smoke on or near the Client's premises;
- Not discriminate against any Client on grounds of ethnicity, religion, gender, disability, or any other protected characteristic;
- Not engage in any conduct that brings the Platform into disrepute;
- Activate the emergency button only in genuine emergencies.

### 5.8 Prohibited Activities

You agree NOT to:

- Solicit Clients for off-platform jobs originated through MyShop;
- Manipulate the App, GPS, or status flow;
- Submit false bids or repeatedly underquote with intent to supplement;
- Permit any unauthorised person to use your Artisan Account;
- Carry out work outside your declared categories or beyond your competence;
- Carry unlawful items onto a Client's premises;
- Engage in any unsafe practice that endangers the Client, household, or yourself.

## 6. Cancellations, Scheduled Jobs, and No-Shows

### 6.1 Artisan-Side Cancellations

**Three (3) cancellations within a rolling 30-day period** triggers a temporary suspension pending admin review. A reason is required for each cancellation. The threshold applies equally to on-demand and advance-scheduled Jobs.

### 6.2 Scheduled Jobs — Confirmation Chain

For Jobs scheduled in advance, you will receive:

- **T minus 24 hours:** a confirmation push asking you to confirm attendance. **If you do not confirm within 4 hours (i.e. by T-20h), admin will be alerted to find a replacement.**
- **T minus 2 hours:** a reminder push.
- **T plus 30 minutes (you have not marked en route):** the Job auto-escalates to the admin queue and the Client is notified that an alternative is being arranged.

A scheduled-job no-show counts as a cancellation under Section 6.1.

### 6.3 Client-Side Cancellations and Compensation

Where a Client cancels after the 30-minute free-cancellation window or after you have marked "en route" (whichever is sooner), a cancellation fee equal to **20% of the agreed price** is payable to you as compensation for time and travel.

## 7. Payment, Commission, and Payouts

### 7.1 When You Are Paid

Payment is released to you after **dual confirmation** — both you and the Client have confirmed Job completion. If the Client does not confirm within four (4) hours of you marking complete, you may escalate to admin, who may force-release payment after reviewing evidence (photos, chat history, timestamps).

### 7.2 Commission

Gilmore Technologies deducts a **platform commission of 20%** from the gross amount of each completed Job. Commission is calculated on the **pre-promo amount** — that is, the agreed price (including any approved supplement) before any client-side promo or discount is applied.

### 7.3 Gateway Fees

Flutterwave gateway fees are absorbed by the Platform within the 20% commission — you are not charged separately.

### 7.4 Payout Options

You may choose between:

- **Instant payout:** earnings transferred to your registered MoMo wallet within approximately 30–60 seconds of dual confirmation; or
- **End-of-day batch payout:** earnings paid in a single transfer at 18:00 GMT.

Both options are **free of charge** to you.

### 7.5 Tips

Tips from Clients are processed separately and **no commission is deducted** from tips.

### 7.6 Disputes and Clawbacks

- A Client may raise a dispute within two (2) hours of completion. During dispute review, **only the disputed Job's amount is frozen** — your other earnings remain accessible.
- If a refund is approved after you have already been paid, the amount may be **clawed back from your next payout**, with notice and explanation.
- Where you fail to perform, perform poorly, or breach these Terms, we may withhold or claw back earnings as appropriate.

### 7.7 Outstanding Balances on Account Closure

- Your account cannot be deactivated or soft-deleted while an outstanding clawback balance exists.
- Balances under **GHS 100** on accounts inactive for more than 90 days may be written off by the Platform.
- Balances **above GHS 100** on inactive accounts will be escalated for manual resolution and may include direct contact and/or referral to a collections process.

### 7.8 Tax

You are solely responsible for declaring and paying all taxes (income tax, VAT where applicable) and statutory contributions on your earnings. We may issue earnings statements to assist your reporting and may share data with the Ghana Revenue Authority where required by law.

### 7.9 Batch Payout Failures

If the daily batch payout fails at 18:00 GMT, automatic retries occur at 19:30, 20:00, and 06:00 the following morning. If all retries fail, admin will be alerted and you will be notified — funds will not silently roll into the next day's batch.

## 8. Performance, Ratings, and Standards

### 8.1 Mutual Ratings

Clients rate you after each Job. Ratings are revealed after both parties submit, or after 24 hours has elapsed (the "blind rating window"), whichever is sooner.

### 8.2 Cancellation Threshold

### 8.3 Rating Threshold

- A rating below **3.5 stars** (over a minimum of 15 completed Jobs) triggers an in-app warning.
- A rating below **3.0 stars** (over a minimum of 15 completed Jobs) triggers automatic suspension pending admin review.

### 8.4 Supplement Frequency

Repeated supplement requests, particularly where bids appear to underquote intentionally, may be flagged and may result in suspension.

### 8.5 Rating Integrity

Ratings procured through inducement, intimidation, or off-platform pressure are a breach of these Terms and may be invalidated.
